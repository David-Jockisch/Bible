In His Arms

"And He took them in His arms and began blessing them, laying His hands on them." Mark 10:16

F.S.B.C. is starting a new ministry!

"In His Arms" will be an umbrella name for several new projects: Blessing Bags, Disaster Relief, and Kids Clothes Swap

Blessing Bags

We have several Blessing Bags that have been donated for people in immediate need for food. (Non-parishable food items.)
This is not a food pantry.

**This will be available as a Community Outreach.

Disaster Relief

We will have several sizes of sweatpants, t-shirts and a few toiletries available for an immediate need for disaster victims such as a natural disaster or a house fire.

**This will be available as a Community Outreach.

Kid's Clothes Swap

April 5th we will be starting a childrens clothing swap project. Parents can bring up to 7 items of their kids clothing and swap them out for up to 7 items from the closet.

Since this is a brand new project, we are offering this service for attendees of the FSBC only (at this time) and will be available after worship on most Sundays. All clothes need to be in good condition, clean and ready to be added to the closet.

** No toys or other items like cribs, walkers, etc.

** Clothing items, shoes, boots, coats only

** Socks and underwear must be new in package
