Baptist Children's Home and Family Services

Since 1918, Baptist Children's Home and Family Services (BCHFS) has been a source of hope and healing, strenghtening families across Illinois by sharing hope in Christ. For the parents whose teen is struggling, the child whose home is no longer safe, the young women facing an unexpected pregnancy, the infertile couple whose greatest dream is to be a Mom and Dad, or the individual family in need of counseling, BCHFS is here to help. The five ministries that make up our mission: Angels' Cove Maternity Home, Baptist Children's Home, Faith Adoption Services, GraceHaven Pregnancy Resource Clinic, and Pathways Counseling.
